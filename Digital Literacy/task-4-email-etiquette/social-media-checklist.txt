Social Media Checklist 

  DO's 

Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings
Think before posting and share responsible content
Verify information before sharing to avoid fake news
Maintain a professional and respectful tone online
Use social media for learning, networking, and growth

  DONT's

Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
Don’t spread rumors or unverified content
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or harmful comments
Don’t click on suspicious links or messages
Don’t overshare content that could harm your digital reputation
